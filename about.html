About us

The first ever online, interactive, educational quantum physics experience. Inspired by the brilliant minds you see in the photo above, Schrödinger's Classroom aims to immerse you in the world of physics like never before. From Schrödinger's Equation and Young's Double-Slit Experiment to Pauli's Exclusion Principle and spin-orbit couplings, you will explore the many diverse theories and experiments of quantum mechanics through our endless selection of lessons that include readings, diagrams, flash games and much more! Once you are comfortable with the content, there are many challenging problems for you to solve and test your knowledge.

Schödinger's Classroom was originally created in November 2017 as a website design project by Cameron Naraine and Darren Strickland. Its goal is to educate users in the physics subfield of quantum mechanics and share the overall joy and passion of physics with the general public.

Please enjoy Schödinger's Classroom and spread the word about it to anyone interested in physics! If you have any questions or concerns about the website, please contact us. We would love to hear your feedback!
